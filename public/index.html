FAQs
How fresh are these gas prices?

Our backend scrapes live pricing feeds. Prices refresh continuously throughout the day, logging a localized timestamp directly inside each marker's information popup.

What if a price on the map is wrong?

Gas station owners can change corporate street signs rapidly. Tap any pricing marker bubble to click the built-in "Flag Report" element to ping our queue for verification parameters.

Why can't I see a specific station?

Check your preferences. It's likely hidden by an active filter parameter—such as your Maximum Price Cap threshold or selected Search Radius boundary settings.

Are these cash or credit card prices?

Our engine maps the absolute lowest rate available. If a location carries different parameters, we default explicitly to cash values to secure the best look on the tracking pills.

Why isn't the "Near Me" button locating me?

Your browser or mobile device might be blocking location permissions. Tap the lock icon in your browser's address bar, enable 'Location access,' and refresh the page to let the map glide to your spot.

Does the map show Midgrade and Diesel?

Yes! While the map default is Regular, you can open your sliding filter form panel or change your permanent account Preferences to instantly swap all map pins to view Midgrade, Premium, or Diesel prices.

Does this app work outside the US?

Currently, our scraper nodes optimize for the United States and parts of North America. We are steadily expanding our database connectivity to track European and global fuel networks soon.

Are Electric Vehicle charging rates tracked?

This feature is on our immediate horizon line! We are actively designing a complementary data layer to let users instantly map local EV charging kilowatt costs alongside combustion fuel nodes.
